About Me

I am currently working on non-Hermitian physics, with a focus on phenomena unique to this field, such as the non-Hermitian skin effect, exceptional points, and spectral topology. More recently, my research has centered on the anomalous dynamics induced by inhomogeneous dissipation, and the characterization of higher dimensional skin effects.
